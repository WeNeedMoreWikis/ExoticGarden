Plants and Fruits

Plants and Fruits are broken up into various categories depending upon what the Plant looks like when it's ready to be harvested.
All plants when initially planted, and when not ready for harvesting (with the exception of trees), are oak saplings, which can make differentiating hard.
The 4 categories of Plants are:

  • Bushes - Single Leaf Blocks
  • Plants - A Single Fruit
  • Vines - A Fruit on top of a Leaf Block
  • Trees - A Full Tree, with multiple Fruit growing from it.

Bushes, Plants, and Vines, are all "single-use". When you right click on the Fruit or Leaf Block, they will return to Sapling Forn and drop 1 Fruit.
Trees, however, drop Fruit when the Fruit is directly clicked, in which case it will re-grow after a period, or when the tree is cut down. Fruit trees when cut will drop multiple sapplings, making them the only plants that can be directly duplicated.
